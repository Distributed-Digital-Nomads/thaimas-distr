DC-MSPP — Decentralized Multi stream promotion plan.

Crowdfunding is the process of jointly launching the production of a product by a community of independent individuals without registering a company. The product can be both tangible and intangible. In the case of BomEbo, it is a software product.

Independent Promo Manager (IPM) — an individual, a participant in this promotion, who used a starting promotional code of the form 1-top- to participate in the promotion and activated his promo code yourself.

Escrow. For the purpose of this promotion, this is the individual or company that is responsible for using the funds from the crowdfunding campaign for its intended purpose. In the case of an individual, this is usually the regional accountant of the BomEbo team.
